# **App Name**: NextVerse Education

## Core Features:

- Video Search: Search for educational videos on YouTube based on user queries.
- AI Summary Generation: Generate summaries of YouTube video transcripts using Gemini 2.5 Flash.
- AI Quiz Generation: Generate quizzes based on the content of YouTube video transcripts, using Gemini 2.5 Flash.
- Course Progression: Lock/unlock topics based on user quiz scores (75% required to unlock the next topic).
- Topic Exploration: Display topics under various departments of learning in card format with images and descriptions.
- Interactive Landing Page: Engage users with a space-themed landing page with animations and glowing buttons for 'Get Started' and 'Explore'.
- Dynamic Search Bar: Let the AI be a tool that selects resources based on content in your search using your courses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep violet (#6A4EBA) evoking a sense of knowledge, curiosity, and the vastness of space.
- Background color: Dark slate gray (#2A2D34), a desaturated violet, providing a dark, immersive backdrop for the space theme.
- Accent color: Electric indigo (#6F38C5), an analogous violet, used for interactive elements to highlight them without overwhelming the interface.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a clean, modern, and readable interface.
- Space-themed background on the landing page with blinking stars and falling meteors to create an immersive experience.
- Subtle neon glow around the edges of buttons and interactive elements.
- Animated growth effect when clicking on subjects or topics, revealing related images and descriptions.
